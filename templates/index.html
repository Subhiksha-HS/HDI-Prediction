🌍 Human Development Index Prediction System

Human Development Index (HDI) is a statistical measure developed by the United Nations Development Programme (UNDP) to evaluate the development level of a country.

HDI is based on three important dimensions:

  • ❤️ Health – Life Expectancy at Birth
  • 📚 Education – Mean Years of Schooling and Expected Years of Schooling
  • 💰 Standard of Living – Gross National Income (GNI) Per Capita

HDI Categories

  • 🟢 Very High Human Development
  • 🟡 High Human Development
  • 🟠 Medium Human Development
  • 🔴 Low Human Development
